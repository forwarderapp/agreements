# 2. Information We Collect

## 2.1 SMS Data

forwardX accesses SMS messages *only* to provide the core functionality explicitly enabled by the user.

SMS data is used for:
- Filtering messages (e.g. OTP or user-defined keywords)
- Forwarding selected messages to a user-specified email address

SMS messages:
- Are processed temporarily on the device  
- Are transmitted securely to our backend server  
- Are **not permanently stored**  
- Are **not logged**  
- Are **not used for advertising or analytics**

All SMS data is transmitted using encrypted **HTTPS (TLS)** connections.
